Detail for CIP Code 46.0505

  • Print

Title: Blasting/Blaster.

Definition: A program that prepares individuals to apply technical knowledge and skills in using a variety of explosive materials to aid in the construction process. Includes instruction in safety procedures for storing, handling, placement, charge power determination, drilling, pounding, building demolition, preparing rocky surfaces for building foundations and demolition of explosives.

Action: No Substantive Changes
