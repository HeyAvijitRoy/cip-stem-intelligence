Detail for CIP Code 42.2816

  • Print

Title: Somatic Psychology.

Definition: A program that focuses on a holistic approach to solving interpersonal issues and psychological problems. Includes instruction in authentic movement, body-mind balance and integration, contact improvisation, dance, Pilates, Tai Chi, relaxation techniques, and Yoga.

Action: New
