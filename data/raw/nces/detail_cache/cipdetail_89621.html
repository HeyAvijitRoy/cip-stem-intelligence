Detail for CIP Code 13.1209

  • Print

Title: Kindergarten/Preschool Education and Teaching.

Definition: A program that prepares individuals to teach students in formal settings prior to beginning regular elementary school, usually ranging in age from three to six years (or grade one), depending on the school system or state regulations. Includes preparation to teach all relevant subject matter.

Action: No Substantive Changes
