Detail for CIP Code 04.0802

  • Print

Title: Architectural Conservation.

Definition: A program that focuses on the architectural design principles and building techniques used in historic structures and environments, and the process of saving and restoring old buildings and districts for contemporary use and enjoyment. Includes instruction in architectural history, building conservation techniques, and material conservation. See also: 30.1201) Historic Preservation and Conservation.

Action: New
