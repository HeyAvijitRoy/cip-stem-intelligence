Detail for CIP Code 30.1701

  • Print

Title: Behavioral Sciences.

Definition: A program with a combined or undifferentiated focus on the social sciences, psychology, and biomedical sciences to study complex problems of human individual and social growth and behavior. See also: 42.0101) Psychology, General.

Action: No Substantive Changes
